The Neediest Cases


 The 104th annual campaign of The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund ran through Jan. 22, 2016. Each profile illustrated the stories of those who benefited from the fund and showed the difference that even a modest amount of money can make.You can donate to the Neediest Cases Fund online or by texting “Neediest” to 732-338-7124.
