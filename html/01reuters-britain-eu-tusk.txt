EU's Tusk Says to Present New Proposal in Britain Talks

By REUTERSFEB. 1, 2016, 1:09 P.M. E.S.T.

BRUSSELS —  European Council President Donald Tusk said he would present on Tuesday a "new settlement" proposal in talks with Britain on reforms to help keep the country in the 28-nation bloc."Tomorrow around noon I will table proposal for a new settlement for #UKinEU. Good progress last 24 hours but still outstanding issues," Tusk said on Twitter.(Reporting by Gabriela Baczynska, Editing by Barbara Lewis)
