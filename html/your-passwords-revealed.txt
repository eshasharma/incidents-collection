Your Passwords Revealed

By IAN URBINAMARCH 10, 2015



Continue reading the main story
Share This Page

Photo







Credit
            Luis Dourado        


Late last year, I wrote an article for the magazine, “The Secret Life of Passwords,” in which I looked at the humanity that often hides in a simple string of characters, and reflected on why we imbue these codes with meaning even when we’re explicitly told not to. A motivational mantra, a swipe at the boss, an ode to a lost love, an inside joke with ourselves, a defining emotional scar — there was something captivating, inspiring even, about these tchotchkes of our inner lives.
