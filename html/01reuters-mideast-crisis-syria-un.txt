GENEVA —  A delegation from Syria's main opposition group, the High Negotiations Committee, arrived at the United Nations in Geneva on Monday for a first official session in U.N.-mediated peace talks overseen by U.N. Syria envoy Staffan de Mistura.The session is a prelude to the start of "proximity talks" that De Mistura hopes to hold between the opposition and a government delegation.(Reporting by John Irish, editing by Tom Miles)
