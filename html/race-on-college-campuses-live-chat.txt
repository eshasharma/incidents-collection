Race on College Campuses


Join New York Times journalists for a live conversation about race on campuses here on this page on Thursday, Feb. 4, at 3 p.m. E.S.T.
FEB. 1, 2016








Struggles over racism and bigotry have ignited protests at colleges across the United States. Are you involved with the issue or wrestling with race on your campus? Or have you witnessed or experienced racism on your campus?
Join New York Times journalists for a live conversation about race on campus here on this page on Thursday, Feb. 4 at 3 p.m. ET.
We’ll have student activists from Princeton, the University of Missouri and other colleges. We’ll have administrators, faculty – and we’re hoping you’ll share your experiences and thoughts as well.
Check back for details on how to take part.
