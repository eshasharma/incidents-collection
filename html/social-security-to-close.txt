'Social Security' to Close
Published: March 19, 1987









                    ''Social Security,'' a comedy by Andrew Bergman about a married couple who are art dealers, will close after the Sunday matinee at the Barrymore Theater. Directed by Mike Nichols, the play will have run for 385 regular performances and 26 previews.
