Afghan Suicide Bomb Kills 10, Wounds 20: Deputy Minister

By REUTERSFEB. 1, 2016, 5:45 A.M. E.S.T.


Inside

KABUL —  Ten people were killed and 20 wounded, most of them civilians in Monday's suicide bomb attack outside a police station in a busy area of western Kabul, Afghan Deputy Interior Minister Ayub Salangi said in a Twitter post.The attack was the latest in a series to hit the Afghan capital this year as the Kabul government has pushed to revive a stalled peace process with the Taliban that broke down last year.(Reporting by Mirwais Harooni; Editing by Robert Birsel)
