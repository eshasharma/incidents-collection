Ganassi Signs Former F1 Driver Max Chilton for 2016 Season

By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 1, 2016, 11:01 A.M. E.S.T.


Inside









Advertisement

INDIANAPOLIS —  Former Formula One driver Max Chilton will drive the full IndyCar Series season for Chip Ganassi Racing.Team owner Chip Ganassi made the announcement Monday, saying Chilton will pilot the No. 8 Chevrolet in 2016. Sage Karam and Sebastian Saavedra drove that car last year.The addition of Chilton means Ganassi will maintain a four-car lineup that includes defending series champion Scott Dixon, Tony Kanaan and Charlie Kimball.Chilton made 35 starts in F1 between 2013 and 2014, with a best finish of 12th, and drove a partial season in Indy Lights in 2015. He notched one win, six top-three finishes and 10 top-five results in the IndyCar feeder series.Ganassi says the 24-year-old driver "brings a good deal of experience" to the team's lineup.
